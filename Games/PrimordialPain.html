Primordial Pain

Primordial Pain is a super difficult area shooter inspired by the likes of Devil Daggers and especially Hyper Demon.
It is built around a core mechanic where the players health is inversely linked to their damage output.
So the lower on health one is, the more damage one does. This results in an intense game of balancing health with maximum damage.
Primordial Pain features psycheledic visuals and a captivating break-core OST.
The game can be found here

Creating this game was a really refreshing experience since most of the visuals were achieved using shaders, which meant lots of graphics programming. It was also the first time I created an online leaderboard system using the SteamAPI, as well as a built in replay system, which allows people to view other players runs and inspect the strategies they employ.
