The Heilwald Loophole

The Heilwald Loophole is a retro horror game set in a run down german hospital.
It tries to subvert the classic trope of being chased around by crazy doctors by adding branching and looping narrative branches, a twisted sense of humor and an awful lot of toilets into the mix.
The game can be found here

The Heilwald Loophole is a super ambitious project with an average playtime of 2-3 hours. It was my first attempt to implement a proper narrative into a game. This definitely shines when meeting the different characters found in the game.
They all have very different personalities, yet all are quite disturbing in their own way.
This project definitely taught me a lot in terms of narrative and environment design.
It once again features a complete OST to accompany the various areas, characters and story moments in the game.
